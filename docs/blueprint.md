# **App Name**: THILELI

## Core Features:

- Amazigh Heritage Storefront: A high-end, responsive landing page featuring a modern masonry grid for Algerian traditional products like Berber rugs, jewelry, and pottery.
- Admin Prestige Dashboard: A professional management interface to oversee orders, manage product stock, and categorize items seamlessly.
- Anti-Fraud Customer Signal: An AI-powered risk assessment tool that identifies potential fake orders by tracking IP patterns and phone numbers, allowing admins to 'signal' or blacklist problematic customers.
- Smart Inventory & Categories: Flexible database storage for product details, image URLs, and dynamic categorization across different cultural lines.
- Integrated Logistics Manager: A customized order form specifically designed for the Algerian market, including selection of the 58 Wilayas and local shipping options.
- Dynamic Support & Social Linker: Context-aware WhatsApp floating support button and social media link management for Facebook, Telegram, and TikTok.
- Sales Analytics Engine: Real-time statistics for daily sales, order statuses, and performance tracking directly from the Cloud database.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Royal Forest Green (#006233), reflecting the natural landscape of North Africa and stability.
- Accent Color: Burnished Gold (#D4AF37), used for the Amazigh 'Zay' (ⵣ) symbol, buttons, and high-end highlights to evoke luxury and heritage.
- Background: Antique Cream (#FDFBF7), providing a warm, traditional feel that contrasts sharply with the deep green and gold elements.
- Headline Font: 'Playfair Display' (Serif) for a high-end, elegant editorial feel. Body Font: 'Inter' (Sans-serif) for clean readability of product descriptions.
- Elegant minimalist icons using 'Lucide-React' combined with custom SVG patterns of traditional Kabyle/Amazigh geometric designs.
- A refined masonry grid for products with wide margins and generous whitespace to emphasize the premium nature of the goods.
- Sophisticated entrance animations and micro-interactions for button hovers and category switching to provide a 'boutique' shopping experience.
